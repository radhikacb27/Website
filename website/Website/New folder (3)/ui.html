Udupi is a coastal town in Karnataka, known for the Sri Krishna Temple,
Udupi cuisine, and beautiful beaches like Malpe.
It is also home to Manipal, a major educational hub.
The town is a mix of spirituality, learning, and nature.

Must Try Food

Masala Dosa

A crispy, golden dosa stuffed
with a flavorful potato filling, served with
chutney and sambar.

Idli-Sambar

Soft steamed rice cakes served
with spicy sambar and coconut chutney.

Gudbud

A popular Udupi dessert made with
layers of different ice cream flavors,
fruits, jelly, and nuts

Goli Baje

Deep-fried, fluffy fritters made from
maida (refined flour), yogurt, and spices.

Mangalore Buns

Sweet and soft banana puris,
best enjoyed with coconut chutney.

Neer Dosa

Thin, soft rice crepes served
with coconut chutney and curry.
